FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Which engagement model is right for me?

It depends on your needs. If you need ongoing, day-to-day accounting and finance support, the Full-Time Growth Partner model is ideal. If your workload is irregular or occasional, Pay As You Go offers maximum flexibility. For defined projects with a clear scope — such as a cleanup or audit — Fixed Budget Engagement works best.

Can I switch between engagement models?

Yes. As your business grows or your requirements change, we can transition you between models with minimal disruption. We'll work with you to find the most suitable arrangement at each stage.

How does the Full-Time Growth Partner model work?

You get a dedicated finance professional embedded into your operations — handling your bookkeeping, reporting, payroll, and advisory needs on a continuous basis. They work as an extension of your team, aligned to your tools, processes, and timelines.

Is there a minimum commitment for Pay As You Go?

Pay As You Go is designed to be flexible — there's no long-term commitment required. You can engage us for a specific task, a short-term project, or on an as-needed basis without any ongoing obligation.

How is a Fixed Budget Engagement scoped and priced?

We begin with a discovery call to understand your requirements, then provide a clear proposal outlining the scope, deliverables, timeline, and cost. Everything is agreed upfront — no surprises, no scope creep without your approval.

What accounting platforms do you support?

We work natively across QuickBooks Online, Xero, Sage, Zoho Books, AppFolio, Yardi, NetSuite, and more. If you use a different platform, just let us know — we're highly adaptable.

Who will I be working with?

You'll have a dedicated point of contact — a qualified Chartered Accountant or finance professional who understands your business and takes full ownership of your engagement from start to finish.

How do I get started?

Book a free 30-minute consultation and we'll take it from there. We'll understand your needs, recommend the right engagement model, and outline the next steps — no pressure, no obligation.
